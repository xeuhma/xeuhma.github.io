Covid story

This is the story of a gas mask that grew up during demonstrations, under tear gas and stone throwing.
Its natural habitat was an urban chaos inhabited by angry men and women fighting for political and social change. Violence, the frantic pace of the city and pollution eventually exhausted him. Forced to leave his preferred environment, he found refuge in the southwest of France in Moliets and Maa.
For him, this lockdown is a break in an eventful life, where time no longer exists and neither does the struggle. Living in harmony with his human companions and helping them in their daily life is his only concern. Introducing simplicity and living a calmer life are his goals.
With the help of the inhabitants of the house, he tells about their daily activities, their small tasks, their life.
